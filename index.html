Beinan's recipe for Pecan Brownies!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 4 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 8 oz semisweet chocolate, coarsely chopped
  • 1 cup chopped pecans

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven for 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Grease a 13x9 inch pan.
  3. In a pan over low heat, melt butter.
  4. Blend in sugar, vanilla, and eggs.
  5. Add flour, cocoa, and salt into the pan, and mix well.
  6. Stir in chocolate and pecans, and mix well.
  7. Pour the batter into the greased pan.
  8. Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 30 to 40 minutes. Cut into bars.
  9. Enjoy!
